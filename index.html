BCIT Tech Collider

I worked with Promp Inc.(ReachXR) to create applications for the 32ft by 8ft motion tracking screen in the BCIT downtown campus tech collider. I created a system to map the tracking information onto ReadyPlayerMe avatars so people can see themselves on the screen, as well as a few miscellaneous apps and games.
